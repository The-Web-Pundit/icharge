BENEFITS

HEALTHY BLOOD SUGAR MANAGEMENT

Each brew contains healthy extracts of Mucuna Pruriens - a potent antidiabetic source. With increasing rates of incidence and mortality on account of diabetes across geographies, diabetes is rightly called as one of the greatest ‘killers’ of health and peace.

Unlike its other counterparts, i-Coffee delays glucose absorption into the blood improves glycemic control, regulates the impaired sugar levels and also helps prevent complications of diabetes.

HEALTHY WEIGHT MANAGEMENT

Known as a ‘wonder herb’, i-Coffee contains extracts of Salacia Reticulata - a plant that has proven to treat early diabetes, anti-obesity and hyperglycemia. The plant extract has shown remarkable potential in developing metabolic syndrome.
